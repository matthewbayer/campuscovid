In the midst of a global health crisis, the American higher education system has decided to make a bet that they can keep college students, faculty, and communities safe while many schools host some form of in-person classes for the Fall 2020 Semester.

This website, designed by concerned college students, serves to make it easier for universities, students, and parents to track COVID-19 outbreaks on college campuses as they happen.

Contrary to popular belief, young people are not at all immune to COVID-19. While fatality rates are much lower among young people than the sick or elderly, even mild mild cases can cause serious long-term damage.

We don't need to panic about the situation, but we do need to be mindful of how we protect our students - and our tuition dollars - against irresponsible actions that lead to the spread of COVID-19.
